Bedtime Rush Design Project

It's time to Sleep!
Date

March 2016


Category

Interactive Design, UI Design, Procrastination Research


Details

Bedtime Rush is an app for intense graduate students named 20-40 years old who always put off their sleeping time to explore if the Rewards and Punishment System is effective on bedtime procrastination, evoke feelings of satisfaction after getting over the procrastination and thoughts about turning off mobile devices at once in the context of playing phones at sleeping time.

In this project, I did some research on my potential users and sent them questionnaire to investigate about their procrastination reason.
